How it works

A repeatable system, tuned to the loan type you are pursuing.

01

Loan Program Intake

You identify the loan program and submit a structured intake form. The form is tailored to your program and collects all the specific information that lender requires before the first draft begins.

02

Writer Assignment

Your file is assigned to a writer with experience in your specific loan type. CSBFP files go to writers who know chartered bank review. SBA files go to writers familiar with US underwriting standards.

03

Draft and Model

Narrative and financial model are written in parallel and integrated before delivery. The financial model format is adapted to the lender: BDC, CSBFP, and SBA each have different projection expectations.

04

Delivery and Revision

Final PDF and editable source files delivered to your inbox. One revision round included on Standard and Comprehensive plans, turned around within 12 hours of your feedback.
